Study Smarter

Your Ultimate Academic Companion

This app is designed to make your study planning simpler and more effective. With features such as study timers, class management, and progress reports, it helps you achieve the best results in your learning journey. The app includes a Persian calendar, offering daily functionality to keep track of your schedule, alongside weekly reminders for lessons to help you stay organized and consistent. The study timer is perfect for setting intervals for studying, taking tests, and other academic tasks, ensuring you stay focused and efficient throughout your study sessions. Additionally, it allows you to log reports in the form of pie charts, providing a visual overview of your progress. The app supports both Persian and English languages, allowing for a personalized experience depending on your preference. It also includes features like checklists and notes for better organization, along with a night study mode designed to reduce eye strain during late-night sessions. Enjoy an ad-free experience, allowing you to focus solely on your studies without interruptions. In addition to these features, the app aims to be a comprehensive tool for students, simplifying and enhancing their academic routines.
